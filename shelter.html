Shelter
+Resettlement Agencies:

Through the admissions process, refugees are resettled according to the State Department. All refugees are already given a place to live before arrival.

+Joining Family:

If a refugee already has family in the US, then the family must let the State Department know when the RSC first accepts the application. The refugee will also be asked about having family in the U.S. in the form to the RSC.
