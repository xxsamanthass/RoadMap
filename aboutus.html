About Us

Hello. My name is Samantha Bhuiyan. I am a rising senior at Bard High School Early College in Queens. I am a Student Ambassador and Peer Tutor for all subjects I've taken. In my free time, I love taking long walks at the park and baking. I joined Girls Who Code because even though I'm indecisive in regards to what I want to do for a career, I think coding is a important skill set to have regardless of what you do because technology is prevalent in every aspect of our society. I wanted to do a project about refugees because I realiazed how difficult it is for refugees to get access to these resources. We wanted the refugees to have access to a website where they could easily understand the appilcation process.
 
Hello. My name is Dea Kurti. I am a rising junior at Brooklyn Technical High School in Brooklyn. I am the Student Assembly Speaker and recently started a project called Novel Girls, which donates books about women empowerment to women around the world. In my free time, I enjoy playing volleyball and sleeping. I joined Girls Who Code, because I wanted to get a new perspective of computer science. After taking a digital electronics class, I realized that I wanted to learn more about coding. I wanted to do a refugee project, because both of my parents are immigrants. They always talk about how difficult it was for them to come to the US, so I was inspired by them.
